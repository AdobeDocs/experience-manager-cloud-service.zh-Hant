## 特性與功能 {#features}

<div class="features-section">
  <h3>表單建立與製作 {#form-creation}</h3>
  <div class="feature-category">
    <div class="feature-item">
      <h4>調適型表單</h4>
      <p>建立並管理互動式、動態、回應式、適合行動使用且資料導向的表單：</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-cloud-service/content/forms/adaptive-forms-authoring/authoring-adaptive-forms-foundation-components/create-an-adaptive-form-on-forms-cs/creating-adaptive-form.html">建立最適化表單</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-cloud-service/content/forms/adaptive-forms-authoring/authoring-adaptive-forms-foundation-components/create-an-adaptive-form-on-forms-cs/themes.html">設定最適化表單的樣式</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-65/forms/adaptive-forms-basic-authoring/create-or-add-an-adaptive-form-to-aem-sites-page.html?lang=zh-Hant">將最適化表單新增至AEM Sites頁面</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-core-components/using/adaptive-forms/sample-themes-templates-form-data-models-core-components.html">使用現成的主題和範本</a></li>
      </ul>
    </div>

<div class="feature-item">
      <h4>Headless最適化Forms</h4>
      <p>在任何網站、應用程式或非視覺互動中建置並以原生方式呈現表單：</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/experience-manager-headless-adaptive-forms/using/overview.html">Headless最適化Forms簡介</a></li>
        <li>以您偏好的程式設計語言建立表單（例如React）</li>
        <li>以原生方式將表單整合至案頭和行動應用程式、網站和聊天應用程式</li>
        <li>透過表單應用程式重複使用您的專有UI元件</li>
        <li>透過前端靈活性利用Adobe Experience Manager Forms的強大功能</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>

<div class="feature-category">
    <div class="feature-item">
      <h4>適用於Forms的Edge Delivery Services</h4>
      <p>建立並提供具備卓越使用者體驗的高效能表單：</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="/help/edge/docs/forms/universal-editor/overview-universal-editor-for-edge-delivery-services-for-forms.md">使用通用編輯器進行WYSIWYG製作</a> — 用於建立表單的強大視覺介面</li>
        <li><a href="/help/edge/docs/forms/create-forms.md">檔案式撰寫</a> — 使用熟悉的工具(例如Microsoft Excel和Google Sheets)建立表單</li>
        <li>用於建立複雜表單邏輯的進階規則編輯器</li>
        <li>透過最佳化的表單載入達到近乎完美的Google Lighthouse分數</li>
        <li>以最短的開發時間以更快的速度部署表單</li>
      </ul>
    </div>

<div class="feature-item">
      <h4>自動化表單轉換服務</h4>

      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/aem-forms-automated-conversion-service/using/configure-service.html">設定自動錶單轉換服務</a></li>

      </ul>

  </div>
